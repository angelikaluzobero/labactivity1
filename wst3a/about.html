About Me

(MY RESUME)

My name is Angelika Luz R. Obero. I was born in Marikina City and raised in Villasis, Pangasinan. I am 20 years old and my birthday is January 18, 2001. I am the youngest in the family and I have a brother and his name is John Benedict. My mother is Ma. Luz Obero and my father is Ben Obero.
I studied Grade School in Barangobong Elementary School and Junior High School at Barangobong National High School. I am consistent honor student. I graduated in Grade School as First Honorable Mention and completed Junior High as second honor. I continued my Senior High School at Don Ramon E. Costales Memorial National High School taking up Technical Vocational Livelihood focus on Computer System Servicing. I graduated in Senior High as With High Honors and passed the National Certificate II in CSS.

Now, I am Third Year College taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and currently studying in Pangasinan State University - Urdaneta City Campus.

My hobbies are watching kdrama and anime. I also love dancing and singing even though I'm not good at it. My inspiration in life is my family of course. I want to be successful to be able to provide them a better life. I also have inspirations which are my idols, Kathryn Bernardo and the Filipino boyband, SB19. They inspire me a lot because they are so successful now. I want to be like them. I love their hardwork in their crafts especially SB19. For me, they are the most humble, hardworking and dedicated in their passion. They inpires me a lot because I, myself, sometimes doubting my ability.

Now, I am a student of IT and I cherish the hope that my diploma will become my ticket to a better tomorrow. As of now my goal is to graduate college in perfect time. And I want to develop my skills and ability to get a better job in the future.
